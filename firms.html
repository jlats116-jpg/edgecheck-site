What this board can't tell you

  • Your win rate is the whole game. These odds assume a trader who genuinely has a 0.125R edge. At a 40% win rate (zero edge) the FTMO figure drops from 76% to ~31%, and the Topstep figure falls further. Run your own numbers before reading any row as "easy".
  • Un-modeled rules bite. Minimum trading days (FTMO 4, FundedNext 5), The5ers' three profitable-days requirement, and Topstep's consistency rule all reduce the odds above by a few points for fast traders. The Topstep browser preset isn't available yet because the site engine doesn't model trailing drawdown — the 47.2% comes from the extended engine in our repo.
  • Passing isn't the finish line. Funded accounts have their own loss limits and payout rules, and a zero-edge trader who passes on variance gives it back later. Check payout track records, not just entry odds.
  • Rules move. Everything here was verified against the firms' published rules and current review sites on 2026-07-16. Confirm on the firm's own site before paying.
